Join Us in Saving Our Oceans from Plastic Pollution

Welcome to etherve, where we're on a mission to combat plastic pollution in our oceans. With over 171 trillion pieces of plastic now adrift in the world's waters, the need for action has never been clearer.

etherve

etherve is a passionate non-profit organization dedicated to tackling the critical issue of plastic pollution in our oceans. Recent estimates reveal a staggering 171 trillion pieces of plastic contaminating our seas, posing a dire threat to marine life, ecosystems, and the health of our planet. This alarming figure represents not just a statistic but a call to action for all who cherish the world's oceans.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to achieve a world where oceans are pristine, free from the devastating impact of plastic pollution. We envision thriving marine ecosystems, teeming with biodiversity, and supporting the health and well-being of all life on Earth. Mission Statement

Our mission is to combat plastic pollution in the world's oceans through innovative cleanup efforts, public education, and advocacy for sustainable practices. We are committed to restoring and protecting marine ecosystems, fostering a global movement towards ocean stewardship, and inspiring communities to take action for a cleaner, healthier planet.
